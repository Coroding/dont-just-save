# Design System

## Style Prompt

A calm 16:9 product demo for the Android app "别只收藏": cream-white paper background, deep navy editorial copy, soft oil-paint color patches, notebook-like cards, and light creator-workflow energy. The piece should feel like a thoughtful portfolio demo, not a loud ad. It uses abstract platform cards instead of real brand logos, and placeholder app mockups instead of real screenshots.

## Colors

- Cream Background: `#FFF8EF`
- Card Cream: `#FFFDF8`
- Deep Navy: `#17203A`
- Sage Green: `#B8C98D`
- Soft Mint: `#BFE3D0`
- Warm Yellow: `#F6C977`
- Soft Pink: `#F6C6D2`
- Mist Blue: `#AFC7E8`
- Lavender: `#D8C7F2`
- Coral: `#F4A78B`

## Typography

- Display and key captions: `LXGW WenKai`, `Microsoft YaHei`, handwritten-friendly fallbacks.
- UI mockup and metadata: `HarmonyOS Sans SC`, `Microsoft YaHei`, sans-serif fallbacks.
- Text should stay large enough for 1920x1080 portfolio viewing: 64px+ headlines, 28px+ body, 20px+ UI labels.

## Motion

- Camera: subtle pushes, small parallax shifts, and gentle card scale changes.
- Scene transitions: warm blur crossfades and soft page-like pushes.
- Element motion: cards slide in, chips pop lightly, state badges morph through scale and color.
- No heavy 3D, no cyber/black-tech look.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use real 小红书/B站/微信 logos or trademarked UI.
- Do not use black cyber backgrounds, neon glows, or hard futuristic grids.
- Do not frame the app as an exaggerated ad; keep it product-thinking oriented.
- Do not depend on real screenshots for this first version.
